Our Story of Beauty & Trust

Roop Nikhar Beauty Parlour was born from a simple belief — every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated. Located in the heart of Ramnagar, Bihar, we've been transforming looks and uplifting spirits for over 5 years.

Our expert team brings years of training and passion to every service — whether it's a simple eyebrow threading or an elaborate bridal transformation.
